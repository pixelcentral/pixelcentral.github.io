Theircraft

Welcome to the Theircraft project site !

Before you can go wild and indulge in all things blocky you need to follow a few simple steps.

Step 1: Setting up Minecraft

Download Minecraft and open the launcher. Sign in and click on "Edit Profile". Under "Use version" choose "1.7.10". Now start the game once. After you find yourself in the main menu close out.

Step 2: Downloading and installing Forge mod loader

Forge is needed to manage and load mods. Download Forge here and simply run the application. Choose "Install client".

Step 3: Downloading and installing mods

Access the dropbox folder and click on the button in the upper right corner to download the mods as a zip-file. Theircraft modpack files

Navigate to your Minecraft installation which should be located under C:\Users\[username]\AppData\Roaming\.minecraft\ Create a new folder named "mods" and copy and paste the contents of the zip-folder in there.

Step 4: Starting for the first time and adjustements

  • Launch Minecraft and under "Edit Profile" make sure to use the version named "release 1.7.10-Forge10.13.4.1614-1.7.19"
  • Under "JVM arguments" copy and paste the following: "-Xmx4G -Xms4G" you can change the number higher values too depending on how much RAM you have (between 4 and 8 is the sweet spot)
  • Hit "Play" to launch the game. This process may take up a few minutes since it actually loads over 100 mods.
  • Ignore all update-related messages

Step 5: Accessing the server

If you are whitelisted simply click on "Multiplayer" and under "Add server" type in "pixelhub.nitrado.net". Now all you have to do is save it and hit connect.

Congratulations !
